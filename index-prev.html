Why Us

Health Guarantee

We understand that you and your family deserve to be protected. For that reason, we provide a one-year health guarantee on every puppy we sell, at no extra charge to you. They are also vet checked, vaccinated and microchipped.

Transportation

We have the experience and knowledge to ensure every puppy gets transported safely to their forever home, whether that's local or an ocean away. We offer lap nanny delivery, local delivery, or we will meet you at a local airport.
